# Leekha Rules

Standard Rules

## Objective

The goal is to **avoid collecting points**.

The game is played until a player reaches **101 points**. That player (and their partner) loses immediately.

## Setup & Deal

- **Deck:** Standard 52-card deck.
- **Cards Per Player:** 13 cards.
- **Dealing Method:** Cards are dealt **one at a time**.
- **Passing Phase:** Before gameplay begins, every player must select **3 cards** to pass to the player on their right.
- **Starting Player:** The player seated to the **dealer's right** leads the first trick.
- **Next Dealer:** The player who collects the **Queen of Spades (Q♠)** in their tricks becomes the dealer for the next round.

## Penalty Points

There are exactly **36 points** in every round.

| Card              | Value     |
| ----------------- | --------- |
| ♥ Any Heart       | 1 Point   |
| ♦ 10 of Diamonds  | 10 Points |
| ♠ Queen of Spades | 13 Points |

## Gameplay Rules

Players must follow the suit of the first card played.

**If you do NOT have the suit led:**

1. You **MUST** play a "Leekha" (10♦ or Q♠) if you have one.
2. If you don't have a Leekha, you may discard any card.

The trick is won by the **highest card** of the suit led. That player leads the next trick.

## Game Over

The game ends the moment a player's total score reaches **101 or more**.

**LOSE:** The team containing the player who reached 101.

**WIN:** The opposing team.
